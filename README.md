# Sai Tunes — Audio Scheduler 🌅🌆

**Sai Tunes** (Aurora Zenith Edition) is a sleek, glassmorphic web application designed to help you organize and schedule your daily audio routines. Whether it's morning meditation music or evening relaxation tracks, Sai Tunes provides a seamless, automated environment to stay in rhythm with your day.

![Sai Tunes Logo](brand-logo.png)

## 🌟 Purpose
The goal of Sai Tunes is to provide a "Zen" audio experience. It automates the transition between your morning and evening audio environments, ensuring that your preferred soundscapes or playlists are ready exactly when you need them, with zero friction.

## ✨ Key Features

### 1. Dual Session Control
- **Morning Playlist**: Optimized for focus and energy (Gold/Amber theme).
- **Evening Playlist**: Optimized for relaxation and wind-down (Cyan/Purple theme).
- **Auto-Play**: Set specific times for each session to start automatically.

### 2. Intelligent Scheduling & Reminders
- **Routine Automation**: Toggle auto-play for sessions to sync with your wake-up or sleep times.
- **Multi-Day Reminders**: Create labels with custom trigger times that persist for days, weeks, or even a full year.

### 3. Premium Audio Player
- **Interactive Visualizer**: Dynamic canvas-based visualizer that dances to your rhythm.
- **Gapless Experience**: Smooth transitions between tracks.
- **Full Control**: Seek, play/pause, skip, and volume adjustments with high-precision sliders.

### 4. Seamless Organization
- **Drag & Drop**: Effortlessly move tracks between Morning and Evening sessions.
- **Fast Uploads**: Batch upload your local audio files directly into the browser.
- **Persistent Storage**: Uses **IndexedDB** to store audio data locally—your playlists are saved even after closing the tab.

### 5. Luminous Aesthetics
- **Aurora Zenith Theme**: A premium glassmorphic UI with vibrant gradients and subtle micro-animations.
- **Luminous Hero**: A centered command hub showing live time and rotating motivational quotes to keep you inspired.

## 🛠 Tech Stack
- **HTML5 & CSS3**: Custom glassmorphic design system using Vanilla CSS.
- **JavaScript (Vanilla)**: Modular architecture for audio management, scheduling, and persistence.
- **IndexedDB**: For high-performance local storage of large audio files.
- **Canvas API**: For the real-time audio visualizer.
- **Web Notifications**: Integration for reminder alerts.

## 🚀 Getting Started
1. Open `index.html` in any modern web browser.
2. **Enable Notifications** using the button in the header to receive session reminders.
3. **Load Samples** or **Add Audio** to populate your playlists.
4. Set your **Trigger Times** and let Sai Tunes handle your daily audio atmosphere.
